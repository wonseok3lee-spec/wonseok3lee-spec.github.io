This blog is where I document what I'm learning at the intersection of **finance, tax, and technology**.

## Why this blog?

Most tax and accounting work still lives in spreadsheets. Most software is built by people without financial domain expertise. I sit in the middle — CPA by training, Tax Consultant by day, systems-builder by night — and I'm building tools that I wish existed.

## What you'll find here

- **Project deep-dives** — notes from building [TaxVista](/projects/taxvista/), [Peep Into Pulse](/projects/peep-into-pulse/), and whatever comes next
- **Data analytics notes** — things I pick up from my bootcamp (Python, SQL, R, Power BI, Tableau)
- **Observations** — on where finance and code meet

Nothing fancy. Just a place to think out loud.

More to come.
