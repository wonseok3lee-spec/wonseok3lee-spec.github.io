Hey, I'm **Wonseok Lee** — a CPA and Tax Consultant by day, a systems-builder by night.

I write, build, and experiment at the intersection of **tax, finance, and technology**. This site is where I collect what I learn along the way.

## What I do

- 🧮 **Day job:** Tax Consultant — domain expertise in individual and international tax
- 📊 **Side work:** Data analytics bootcamp (Python, SQL, R, Power BI, Tableau)
- 🔧 **Projects:** Building tools like [TaxVista](/projects/#taxvista) and [Peep Into Pulse](/projects/#peep-into-pulse)
- 📝 **Writing:** Notes from the bridge between accounting and code

## Why this blog?

Most tax and accounting work is done in spreadsheets. Most software is built by people without financial domain knowledge. I sit in the middle — and I'm building tools that I wish existed.
